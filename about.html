About the Hosts

Kendall Gunkel

Kendall is from Atlanta, Georgia and moved out to Denver in May of 2019 to pursue an opportunity as a Commercial Account Executive with CircleCI. He’s an avid sports fan, most particularly for the Georgia Bulldogs (which you’ll hear plenty about during the episodes). During his free time you can find Kendall at the local comedy club watching an improv show or stand up special. He also has a passion for giving back to the community, most recently volunteering as a mentor at Big Brothers Big Sisters of Colorado.




Eric Taylor

Eric Scott Taylor is too much of a mouthful for any single white man to be named if he really has hopes of standing out and making it, so most people just call Eric, “ET”. After growing up watching too much Laguna Beach in Colorado, ET moved to California for undergrad which thrust him into a technology career in Silicon Valley after graduating. Pairing his nerdy mind with the entrepreneurial culture of San Francisco he launched his first lifestyle business in 2017. Here’s the thing though, businesses can fail, and that’s exactly what happened to his. While reflecting on that loss and living in LA, the city of dreamers, he realized there isn’t enough information out there describing the difficulty of building something from nothing and a fascination with documenting the untold come up story arose. Moving back into his parent’s basement in Colorado to pay off his failed business debt, and to take a full-time opportunity at CircleCI he met Kendall and saw the missing ingredient to bring The Trying to Make It Podcast to the world.
